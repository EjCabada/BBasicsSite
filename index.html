Prep

Whips
  • 4 pumps of Vanila into Streaming Pitcher
  • Heavy Cream to the max line in pitcher
  • Add all content into the Whip Canister
  • Screw on top and screw on Co2 cartridge
  • Shake vigourously(10x) and let it sit
    • Sweet Cream
  • In 2L pitcher add 1L of heavy cream
  • 350ml of Vanilla
  • 2% Milk to 2L line
    • Refreshers
  • Add Juice Concentrate to 1L line in 2L pitcher
  • Add cold water to top
    • Tea
  • Add tea sachel to 2L pitcher
  • Hot Water to 1L line
  • Brew for 5min
  • 1 Venti scoop ice
  • Add Cold Water to 2L line
    • Lemonade
  • Pour Lemonade concentrate to .75 line in 2L pitcher
  • Add cold water to 2L line
    • Mocha
  • Add 2L of hot water into Round 3.5L pitcher
  • Add small amounts of 2lb bag of Mocha slowly and wisk
  • Repeat adding small amounts of mocha and wisking till done
    • Frap Roast
    Ice Coffee
  • (1:1 ratio of ice to iced coffee)
  • Pour ic Coffee into 2L pitcher and add to ic Coffee container
  • 2L of ice into the the Iced Coffee Container
    • Cold Brew
  • Grind full bag of coldbrew beans on [french-press]
  • Pour grounds and coldbrew filter into coldbrew maker
  • Pour 12L of water
  • Set the 20hr timer
    • Cold Brew Kegs
  • Add 4L of water and 2L of ice into cube
  • Wisk till ice is fully melted
  • Add 5L(full cube) of coldbrew concentrate into keg
  • Add iced water into keg
